Product Update

Razorpay & UPI Integration Now Live for All Plans

Accept payments directly from your VITAAN invoice — no manual reconciliation needed. Razorpay and UPI collections are now available across all subscription plans.

S

Sivasubramaniyam V

Founder & CEO

February 2026
4 min read

Payment collection has always been one of the biggest pain points for Indian SMBs — creating an invoice in one system, collecting payment on UPI, then manually reconciling in your accounts. VITAAN now closes this loop completely.

What is now possible

  • Add a Razorpay payment link directly to any VITAAN invoice
  • Accept UPI, net banking, credit/debit cards, and wallets — all from one link
  • Payments are automatically reconciled against the invoice in real time
  • Partial payments are tracked with outstanding balance updated automatically
  • Payment confirmations are sent to customers via email and WhatsApp

How to connect Razorpay

Go to Settings → Integrations → Razorpay. Enter your Razorpay API key and secret. VITAAN will verify the connection and activate payment links on all new invoices. You can also enable it on existing unpaid invoices in bulk from the Finance → Invoices screen.

UPI QR codes

If you prefer UPI QR codes over Razorpay links, VITAAN can generate a static UPI QR code linked to your business VPA. Every payment scanned against this QR is automatically matched to the outstanding invoice using the amount and reference number.

Razorpay and UPI integration is available on all plans at no additional charge. Standard Razorpay transaction fees apply.
