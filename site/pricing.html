Common questions

14-day free trial

Every Pro plan starts with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. Cancel anytime.

Can I try VITAAN before paying?
−
Yes — every plan comes with a 14-day free trial, no credit card required. You get full access to all features in your chosen plan.
What modules are included in Starter?
+
Starter includes any 2 modules of your choice. You can add more modules later as your business grows — no plan change required.
Is VITAAN GST compliant?
+
Yes. VITAAN Finance is fully GST-ready with GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, and GSTR-2A reconciliation built in. E-invoicing is supported for businesses above the threshold.
Does VITAAN support UPI payments?
+
Yes. VITAAN integrates with UPI, Razorpay, and other Indian payment gateways for both receivables and payables out of the box.
Can I migrate from Tally or Excel?
+
Our data migration wizard supports Tally XML exports, Excel spreadsheets, and CSV files. Enterprise customers get a guided migration with our team.
Is my data stored in India?
+
Yes. All data for Indian businesses is stored on AWS Mumbai (ap-south-1). We comply with India's data localization requirements.
