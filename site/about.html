About SyncCore

Built by developers, for developers shipping real software

SyncCore started as an internal tool, built to solve the real operational challenges Indian SMBs face every day.

Every Indian SMB owner is already managing GST, payroll, inventory, and cash flow simultaneously. VITAAN was built so none of that slows you down — because your energy belongs on growing your business, not running it.

Our Story

From internal tool to production platform

01
2022 - 2024 - VITAAN Built

Started as an internal tool to solve real Indian business challenges — GST, UPI payments, multi-entity management, and payroll compliance.

02
2025 - First Customers

Opened VITAAN to early customers. SMBs across India onboarded, product-market fit confirmed.

03
2026 - Mobile & Desktop

Launched native iOS/Android apps and an Electron desktop client. VITAAN now runs on every platform your team uses.

04
2026 - Production Platform

100+ SMBs onboarded. Enterprise tier launched with dedicated instances, custom workflows, and SLA-backed support.

Our Values

What we stand for

Developer First

Every decision is made with developer experience in mind. Clean APIs, clear docs, and TypeScript throughout so integrating VITAAN feels obvious.

Transparent Pricing

No hidden fees, no per-seat taxes, no surprises. You pay for your plan — the metric that actually reflects your business's scale.

Open Core Philosophy

The core platform is built to be self-hostable and auditable. We earn revenue from cloud hosting and enterprise support — not by locking you in.
